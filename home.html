How to book a hostel room?

  1. To make things easy you can first search the availability of hostels in your college from the "Hostels" section present in the navigation bar.
  2. Find your desired hostel then just create an account and book a hostel of your choice.
  3. Once you have booked your hostel the representative or the head of the hostel will get back to you after verifying your details and your application for hostel accomodation will be accepted depending on the availability of hostels.

Features

  1. Pay your hostel and mess fees through secure online payments.
  2. Search for availability of hostels in your college.
  3. Give feedback online regarding quality of food and cleanliness.
  4. You can rate your hostel and the rating will be available to all users and non-users.
  5. You can ask queries related to food, cleanliness and other things directly to your hostel head/warden through our inbuilt messaging system.

From the developers

"YourHostel" has been developed to ease the process of booking hostels in your colleges. Hostel life is something that every college student should experienced in their lifetime. But many students don't take hostels in a fear to get ragged or because of bad food. Here at "YourHostel" we ensure that the hostels provide a safe, secure and a clean environment for all the students. Currently only 1 university has joined with us and plans are underway to integrate many more college hostels, private hostels and even PG's.
